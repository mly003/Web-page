About

Hello, and welcome to my webpage! The theory contained here is intended to give the reader the breadth of understanding required in order to delve into the current research in the theory of strongly correlated electron systems in time varying electromagnetic fields.

Now, the prerequisite is an intermediate/advanced course in quantum mechanics ( the reader should especially be aquianted with basic light matter processes and numerical solutions of more complex systems) as well as a rudimentary understanding of its application to condensed matter physics - in particular, band theory.
