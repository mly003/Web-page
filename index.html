About

Hello, and welcome to my webpage! The theory contained here is intended to give the reader the breadth of understanding required in order to delve into the current research in the theory of strongly correlated electron systems in time varying electromagnetic fields.

Now, the prerequisites include an advanced course in quantum mechanics ( the reader should especially be aquianted with basic light matter processes) as well as a rudimentary understanding of its application to condensed matter physics - in particular, band theory. The reason for mentioning the above is because they lay the foundation for the most imperative background knowledge; namely, quantum condensed matter theory of which some experience with second quantization is the most important.
